Who We Are

Eliminating the Middleman.
Since Day One.

KiAnben was founded with one mission: make importing accessible to everyone in Bangladesh. Import licenses are a major barrier for small and medium businesses — we bridge that gap.

Through our community model, members pool orders and import directly under KiAnben's licensed umbrella. No individual license needed. We handle everything for just 1% commission.
